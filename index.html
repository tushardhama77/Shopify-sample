Details

Made to disappear under your hands

Keystroke One uses a gasket-mount design, so the plate flexes slightly with every press instead of bottoming out hard against the case. Combined with foam dampening, typing sounds rounded and deep rather than clacky.

Every switch socket is hot-swappable, so you can move from a linear to a tactile feel in about ten minutes with the included puller — no soldering iron required.

The 75% layout keeps arrow keys and a function row while cutting the numpad, so it fits in a bag without losing the keys people actually reach for.
